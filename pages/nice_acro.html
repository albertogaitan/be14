← NICE, FRANCE – Acropolis — 2003 (Feb – Mar)

10 February to 10 March blind eye videos played on exoptitrons and banners and other material, including installation gaffers tape, all gathered from previous exhibits and priced for sale filled a 20 foot display at the Nice Acropolis. Window installation offering blind eye works for sale.
