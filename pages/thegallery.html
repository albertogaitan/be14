← BROOKLYN, NYC – www.gallerythe.org – 2002 (Jan – Feb)

The blind eye project joined the surge of NYC’s transient professionals and aspiring artists that had begun increasingly to occupy housing and retail in Carrol Gardens, a predominantly working class Italian neighborhood since 1900. Nancy Meli Walker’s storefront gallery on Smith Street near Carrol, was dedicated to new media.
