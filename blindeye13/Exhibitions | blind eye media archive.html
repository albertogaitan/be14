Bebop Galerie – Nice, France (Jan-March 2003)

Last blind eye exhibit opened with show & discussion at Bebop Galerie, Nice, France – 25 January 2003. Naval Cassidy, also from New York, performed exoptically inspired “live-cinema” and Riviera jazz great, Bunnie Foy, sang. Bebop exhibit ran 25 January – 18 February. Last window opened at Nice-Acropolis 10 February to 10 March 2003. →

Schikaneder cafe-cinema in Vienna, Austria (Jan 2002)

The Schikaneder cafe-cinema is one of the first movie houses in Vienna. It’s window front gives onto its cafe-bar and Margaretenstrasse in the central city. Thanks to Austrian artist Hans Schabus for arranging the space. →

Times Square window at 125 West 42nd Street, NYC (Jan 2002)

Thanks to the Chashama Theater Group of New York, blind eye projects installed a window at 125 West 42nd Street. Blind eye projects also coordinated a performance and opened an art exhibition on the other side of the window installation.
