narration:

"Dear gentle viewer, Through history, moving media have been made to attract attention. Moving media, whether shadow plays or virtual reality programs, have been made to excite and to hold observers. At the very least, they have been created to be observed, to be looked upon.

Exoptic fields breaks with that history. What follows is made to deflect your attention, to make you look away by negating the screen. One desired consequence is to intensify all other sensations: whether sight, sound, touch, smell, taste, or emotion."
